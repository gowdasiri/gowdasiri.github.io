Our Itinerary

How to get there?

Take a flight to Port Blair and then a ferry to and from Havelock. Make sure to book your ferry in advance. We used ITT Majestic to get to Havelock and Macruise to get back. Neither had an outdoor deck that could be used by the passengers. Between the two we liked ITT Majestic more.
